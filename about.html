About

Built for golfers who show up prepared

Every other golf app starts on the first tee. b4u.golf starts the night before. Our goal is simple: help every golfer — first-timer or 30-year regular — walk to the first tee feeling ready.

Our promise

The internet has plenty of golf information. It's just scattered across 200 websites, each making you click 5 times to find one answer. We pull together the things golfers actually check before a round — weather, course info, etiquette, gear, warm-up routines — into one fast page you can pull up in 10 seconds.

Three rules we'll always follow:

  • Free will stay free. The features that make pre-round prep simple won't move behind a paywall.
  • No ads. Not on free, not on Pro. Your time on the course is too short to waste on banners.
  • Honest pricing. $1.99/mo or $14.99/yr — period. No introductory offers that triple after 90 days.

Contact

Questions, feature requests, course corrections, or just want to say hi? We read every email.

📧 hello@b4u.golf

You can also reach us at our backup domain b4ugolf.com — same site, just a different way of typing it in.

For pro shops & course operators: we're rolling out a free course-listing program in 2026. If you'd like b4u.golf to feature your course with conditions, tee-time deep-links, and reviews, email courses@b4u.golf.

Privacy

We use your location only when you ask us to (clicking "find courses near me" or loading the weather page). We don't sell your data — we don't even collect most of it. Anonymous, aggregated usage analytics only. Full policy at /privacy (coming soon).

Disclaimer

b4u.golf is an information and prep service. Weather data is provided by Open-Meteo and shouldn't be relied on for safety-critical decisions — when there's lightning, follow the course's signal and your own judgment. Tee-time bookings are handled by third parties (GolfNow, GolfPass) and are subject to their own terms.
