Frequently asked

Why so cheap?

Most golf apps charge $5/mo or $40+/yr. We think pre-round prep should be accessible to every golfer — not just the ones who already know what slope rating means. We also keep our infrastructure lean (no ads, no bloat) so $1.99 covers our costs and a fair margin.

Will the free tier ever change?

No. The features in the Free Forever column above are guaranteed free for the life of your account. Pro is purely additional.

Can I install it like an app?

Yes. b4u.golf is a Progressive Web App — on iPhone, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". On Android, tap "Install app" in the Chrome menu. It works offline once installed, no App Store download needed.

How do live competitions work?

One player creates a round and gets a 6-letter room code. Anyone in your group joins by entering the code on their phone. Scores update live for all players, with a real-time leaderboard. Skins, Nassau, match play, and scrambles supported.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel from your account page; you keep Pro access until the end of the billing period. No fees, no questions, no "are you sure" gauntlet.

Will there be a native iOS / Android app?

Likely yes, in 2026. We're building the PWA first because it works on every phone today — when we ship to the App Store and Play Store, it'll be the same product, just wrapped natively.
